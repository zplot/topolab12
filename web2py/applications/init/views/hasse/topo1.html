Introduction

Finite T0-spaces are in correspondence with finite partially ordered sets(posets).

A useful way to represent finite T0-spaces is with their Hasse diagrams. The Hasse diagram of a poset X is a digraph whose vertices are the points of X and whose edges are the ordered pairs (x, y) such that x < y and there exists no z âˆˆ X such that x < z < y. In the graphical representation of a Hasse diagram we will not write an arrow from x to y, but a segment with y over x You can use this application to draw Hasse diagrams of finte T0-spaces.
